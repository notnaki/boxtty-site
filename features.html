Features

A keyboard-first Gmail client that runs on your machine. Fast, private, programmable.

  • Real Gmail, instant UI

    Talks to Gmail REST v1 directly — threads, labels, search, send, drafts, attachments. The interface stays optimistic, so archive / star / trash land instantly and reconcile in the background.

  • Push, not poll

    An IMAP IDLE connection per account delivers new mail the moment it arrives — no 60-second refresh wait. Incremental history.list sync keeps everything current cheaply.

  • On-device AI

    Summarize a thread, draft a smart reply, or extract action items — running on Apple Intelligence locally by default (no key, nothing leaves your Mac). Or plug in Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, or a local Ollama model.

  • Snooze & schedule send

    Snooze a thread out of the inbox until later; schedule a message to go out at a chosen time. Both are local and survive relaunch.

  • Programmable filters

    Rules match on sender or subject and auto-archive, trash, mark read, or star arriving mail — applied the instant new threads land.

  • Compose that helps

    Recipient autocomplete from everyone you've corresponded with, shorthand snippet buttons, Markdown shortcuts (**bold**, _italic_, [links](url)), and an undo-send window.

  • Instant search

    Free text plus operators — from:, is:unread, is:starred — combined, e.g. from:linear is:unread deploy. Page in older matches beyond the synced window.

  • Multiple accounts

    Connect several Gmail accounts into one unified inbox. Per-account colour stripes, per-account push, and replies that go out from the right address automatically.

  • Scriptable over a socket

    The running app hosts a Unix-socket control server. Drive it from the boxtty CLI or write Lua 5.4 automations — boxtty.list(), boxtty.archive(id), boxtty.send{…}.

  • Keyboard-first

    Move, open, archive, star, reply, snooze, compose — all from the home row. A ⌘K command palette covers the rest. Remap keys in settings.

  • Yours, on disk

    State persists to ~/.config/boxtty as plain JSON/TOML. Live-watched config.toml re-themes the whole UI from a single accent knob — no relaunch.

Read the documentation to set it up, or get the app.
